How Covid-19 affected the job market

The purpose of this is project is to show how Covid-19 affected some of the aspects of the job market in Europe, by analyzing data from 2020 and before.

  • The aspects that we are interested in are:
  • Remote work or “Smart working” (employees working from home).
  • Job starters and leavers (who starter a new career or quitted a previous one).
  • Unemployment rates (with a particular focus on the relationship with the educational attainment level).

All the data used on project are property of Eurostat, Covid-19 section.
